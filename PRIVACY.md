# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 26, 2026**

## Overview

0xBlog ("we", "our", or "the App") is a blog management application for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

## Information Collection

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal information
- We do not track your location
- We do not use analytics or tracking services
- We do not collect usage statistics
- We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
- We do not use advertising or ad tracking

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device only:

- **Server Configuration**: The host address and port number of your self-hosted blog server (stored in local app preferences)
- **API Key**: Your authentication key for accessing your blog server (stored securely in the iOS Keychain)
- **Display Preferences**: Your dark mode preference (stored in local app preferences)

This data never leaves your device except when the App communicates directly with your self-hosted server.

## Data Transmission

The App communicates only with the server you configure. We do not operate any central servers or cloud services. All data transmission occurs directly between your device and your own self-hosted blog server.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Data Security

- Your API key is stored using the iOS Keychain, Apple's secure credential storage system
- All data remains on your device and under your control
- You can delete all stored data at any time by using the "Disconnect" feature in Settings or by uninstalling the App

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete from our systems. All data stored by the App is local to your device and can be removed by:
1. Using the "Disconnect" option in the App's Settings
2. Uninstalling the App from your device
